About SHIKSHARTHI



Curiosity is the mother of invention but here it is Mother of Miracle. A Miracle that has grown in – SHIKSHARTHI. It is a pioneer institute in the field of innovative coaching methods for Board and Competitive Exams. Founded on April 3rd, 2012, and named with the synonym of ‘Students,’ who are our first priority. Since the year of beginning, we have been creating miracles by holding average marks of more than 90% for all the students of ICSE, CBSE, and ISC boards. As a Science institute, we are cultivating the interest of learning science in the students through our practical classes, brainstorming experiments, online videos, to the point study materials. All the tutors here are highly qualified with dynamic academic records along with high experience in teaching from the top professions the field can provide.
